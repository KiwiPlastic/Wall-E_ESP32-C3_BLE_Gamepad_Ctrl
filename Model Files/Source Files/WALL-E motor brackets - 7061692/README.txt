WALL-E motor brackets by DreadPirateBoberts on Thingiverse: https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:7061692

Summary:
These are modified motor brackets for the Chillibasket WALL-E replica. The brackets allow for a stronger, more secure mounting of the drive motors. They are now one piece design, 1.5 mm longer, provision for two motor mounting bolts. The drive pinion has also been modified to allow for better clearance of the grub screw.
